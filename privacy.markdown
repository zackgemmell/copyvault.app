<p class="text-gray-400 text-lg mb-8"><strong>Effective Date:</strong> November 2, 2025</p>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg mb-12">Copy Vault ("the App") is developed by Zack Gemmell. This policy explains how your information is handled when you use Copy Vault on macOS and its companion app on iOS.</p>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

## <span class="text-white text-3xl font-bold">1. Overview</span>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg">Copy Vault is a clipboard history manager that allows you to securely save, view, and reuse items you copy on your device. Your privacy is our top priority. The App is designed to function entirely on your device, without collecting, transmitting, or storing any personal data externally.</p>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

## <span class="text-white text-3xl font-bold">2. Information We Do Not Collect</span>

<ul class="text-gray-300 text-lg space-y-3">
  <li><strong class="text-white">No Personal Data:</strong> Copy Vault does not collect, store, or share any personal information such as your name, email address, or payment details.</li>
  <li><strong class="text-white">No Clipboard Content Transmission:</strong> Clipboard entries are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to any server controlled by the developer or third parties.</li>
  <li><strong class="text-white">No Analytics or Tracking:</strong> Copy Vault does not use analytics tools, tracking software, advertising identifiers, or crash reporting frameworks that collect personal data.</li>
</ul>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

## <span class="text-white text-3xl font-bold">3. Optional iCloud Sync</span>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg mb-4">If you enable iCloud Sync, your clipboard history is securely stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit technology.</p>

<ul class="text-gray-300 text-lg space-y-3">
  <li>The data remains under your Apple ID and is not accessible to the developer.</li>
  <li>Apple's <a href="https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/" class="text-brand-blue hover:underline">Privacy Policy</a> governs all data stored in iCloud.</li>
  <li>Disabling iCloud Sync will stop synchronization and remove cloud copies as controlled by your Apple settings.</li>
</ul>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

## <span class="text-white text-3xl font-bold">4. Data Storage and Security</span>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg">All clipboard data is stored locally on your device (and optionally in iCloud). Your data is never shared, sold, or transmitted to any external server. The App uses standard Apple sandboxing and system-level protections to help secure your information.</p>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

## <span class="text-white text-3xl font-bold">5. Third-Party Services</span>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg">Copy Vault does not integrate with or rely on any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.</p>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

## <span class="text-white text-3xl font-bold">6. Children's Privacy</span>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg">Copy Vault does not target or knowingly collect any data from children under 13 years of age.</p>

<div class="border-t border-gray-800 my-12"></div>

<p class="text-gray-300 text-lg">If this policy changes, an updated version will be posted within the App and on any associated website. The effective date at the top will reflect the latest revision.</p>
